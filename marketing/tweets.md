## Feature Spotlights

**Tweet 4 — Markdown**
```
You already know markdown. Now journal with it.

Journal supports full GitHub-flavored markdown — headers, lists, tables, strikethrough, and more.

Write the way developers and writers think. Format as you go.
```

**Tweet 5 — Split View**
```
Write on the left. See the magic on the right.

Journal's split-view mode lets you edit markdown and watch it render in real time.

No more toggling. No more guessing. Just seamless writing. ✨
```

**Tweet 6 — Auto-Save**
```
There's nothing worse than losing a journal entry mid-thought.

Journal saves everything automatically as you type. Instantly. Always.

Close the tab. Come back tomorrow. Your words will be exactly where you left them.
```

**Tweet 7 — Typography**
```
The font you write in matters more than you think.

Journal uses Lora for prose, JetBrains Mono for code, and Inter for the UI — because beautiful writing deserves beautiful type.

Your words look as good as they feel.
```

**Tweet 8 — Privacy**
```
Zero servers. Zero accounts. Zero data collection.

Journal stores everything locally in your browser. Your entries never leave your device.

In a world of surveillance capitalism, keeping a private journal is a radical act.
```

**Tweet 9 — PWA / Offline**
```
Install Journal like an app. Use it offline. Write anywhere.

No internet? No problem. Journal works completely offline and can be added to your home screen on any device.

Your writing habit shouldn't depend on WiFi.
```

**Tweet 10 — Word Count**
```
Hit your daily writing goal with Journal's built-in word and character counter.

Whether you're doing morning pages, writing 750 words a day, or just tracking your streak — you always know where you stand.
```

## Developer / Tech Audience

**Tweet 18 — Tech Stack**
```
Journal is built with:

→ Next.js + TypeScript
→ Tailwind CSS
→ React Markdown (GFM support)
→ localStorage (no backend needed)
→ PWA-ready with offline support

Simple stack. Beautiful result. Open to contributions. 🛠️
```

**Tweet 19 — Privacy Tech**
```
"Just use [Big App]" they said.

But [Big App] stores your entries on their servers, mines your data for ads, and can lock you out of your own journal.

Journal is 100% client-side. Your data lives in your browser. Ship it to yourself. Own it completely.

```
Journal is minimal by design — but your words, your workflow, your rules.
